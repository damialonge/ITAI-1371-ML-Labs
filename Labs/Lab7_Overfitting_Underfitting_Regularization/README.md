# Lab 7 – Overfitting, Underfitting & Regularization

## Overview
This lab explored one of the most important concepts in ML — how models can fail by memorizing training data (overfitting) or being too simple (underfitting) — and the techniques to fix both.

## What I Learned
- What **overfitting** looks like: high training accuracy, low test accuracy
- What **underfitting** looks like: poor performance on both training and test sets
- The **bias-variance tradeoff** and why it matters
- Regularization techniques: **L1 (Lasso)** and **L2 (Ridge)**
- How to use learning curves to diagnose model performance

## Challenges Faced
Visualizing the bias-variance tradeoff and building intuition for when a model is "just right" took practice. The regularization math was also a new concept to absorb.
